Garlic Knots

Description:

Ingredients

Flour

Yeast

Garlic

Fresh Basil

Parmesan Cheese (optional)

Steps Overview

  1. Step 1: Dry ingredients – Combine 1 cup of flour with the remaining dry ingredients, then stir in the oil and warm water.
  2. Step 2: Gradually add more flour as needed until the dough becomes elastic and cohesive.
  3. Step 3: Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Let rise in a warm spot for 30 minutes or until doubled in size.
  4. Step 4: Deflate and divide – Gently deflate the dough and form it into a 15″ log before cutting into 9 even pieces.
  5. Step 5: Tie into knots – Roll each piece into an 8″ rope, tie in a knot, and place on a parchment lined baking sheet.
  6. Step 6: Brush each garlic knot with olive oil (optional) and let sit for 5 minutes. Bake for 13 minutes at 400F.
  7. Step 7: Brush with garlic butter after baking and sprinkle with grated parmesan just before serving.
