Hot Dog

Description:

Ingredients

1 all-beef hot dog

1 poppy seed hot dog bun

1 tablespoon yellow mustard

1 tablespoon sweet green pickle relish

1 tablespoon chopped onion

2 tomato wedges

1 dill pickle spear

2 sport peppers

1 dash celery salt

Steps Overview

  1. Step 1: Boil hotdog for five minutes.
  2. Step 2: Steam bun until warm.
  3. Step 3: Place hot dog on bun and assemble toppings.
